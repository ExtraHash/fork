Building A Website The Smart Way

Our website is actually hosted for free on Github.com, with no ads or fees. This allows you to have a 100% uptime website that loads quickly and is already set up for multiple people to collaborate together on the design.

Unlike normal websites, our website is written in Markdown, which is more approachable for nerdy people these days than HTML. Github interprets markdown natively, and inserts the appropriate language words into fields of templates you create.

https://github.com/turtlecoin/turtlecoin.lol  Your New Website

Fork this repository, and give the repository the name of the domain you want to point at it.


    https://github.com/turtlecoin/turtlecoin.lol

_i18n/en.yml  English Translation

Assuming you speak english, editing this file first, side by side with a browser tab for your new Github Page, is the easiest way to begin customizing the website. You don't have to use everything on the website in your content, and I'll show you how to modify the main layout in the next section.

The Markdown processor in Github Pages automatically knows to substitude these lines in the special corresponding fields of your layout. Once you are done with your native language translation, consider asking your community to supply their own translations in their language. International translations are critical to building global adoption.


  # https://turtlecoin.lol/

The fields are simple, and the only part that requires editing are the parts that come after the colon.
